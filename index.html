Most common greek weapons

The greeks most common weapon was the spear along with the sword.The spear was so significant because it had medium range and could also be used in hand-to-hand combat. Swords were used as a secondary weapon in the case of a broken spear or if a enemy is in meelee range.

common greek armor

Most warriors only wore a breast plate and helmet.(Witch is made of a silver bronze mixture)but different warriors war different parts of armor like the hoplites wore greaves.(protection for the lower leg)along with a helmet and chestplate

Greek battle formations

The greeks had a very intresting battle formation called the phalanx. In a phalanx formation the first ranks (lines of soilders) of the army have your shields facing forward along with a spear to make a wall with spear tips. "that seems pretty scary to me."
